ABOUT ME

I'm Susana Minagawa, a freelance graphic designer in Argentina. Over the past 9+ years, I’ve used my experience in graphic design to help clients bring their brand and ideas to life. I enjoy working with businesses and any individual that’s also passionate about what they do. When I’m not helping my clients, I enjoy working on my own side projects. This helps me stay fresh in what I do and gives me new challenges to experience.
